Basic Information about Cervical Cancer

Cervical cancer is one of the most preventable and treatable forms of cancer, with almost all cases linked to high-risk human papillomaviruses (HPV).

It develops in a woman's cervix, and almost always caused by HPV, which is transmitted through sexual contact.
